This is the list of functions available on "intrinsics".

- list
- cons
- nth <list> <index>
- len <list>
- idx <el> <list>
- remove <index>
- rev
- sublist
- slice

- is-nil
- type-of (retorna atom)
- assert-type

- println

- ffi/open
- ffi/bind

- and
- or
- not

- if
- cond

- str

- str/substr
- str/append
- str/chars (vec)
- str/atom
- str/parse

- vec/for
- vec/idx
- vec/push
- vec/to-list
